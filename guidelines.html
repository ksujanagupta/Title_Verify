Guidelines for Using the Title Verification System

Follow these instructions to verify or generate a title:

Step 1: Enter Your Title

Enter the title you wish to verify or generate in the input box. The title should be at least 6 characters long. If your title is too short, you will be prompted to try again.

Example: ShivaniMudiga is valid. However, abc will show an error since it is less than 6 characters.

Step 2: Title Verification Rules

  • Disallowed Words: Your title should not contain disallowed words such as "police", "crime", "corruption", etc.
  • Disallowed Prefixes/Suffixes: Prefixes like "The", "India", "News" are not allowed if they result in a title too similar to an existing one.
  • Existing Titles: The system will reject titles that closely resemble existing titles in the database (e.g., "Hindu", "Indian Express").
  • Combining Existing Titles: Creating a new title by combining parts of existing titles is not allowed. For example, if "Hindu" and "Indian Express" exist, "Hindu Indian Express" is not allowed.
  • Variations in Meaning: Titles with similar meanings in different languages or with periodicity modifications (e.g., Daily, Weekly) will also be rejected.

Step 3: Suggestions

If the system finds any disallowed parts in your title, it will provide a suggestion by removing those parts. If the cleaned title is still valid (at least 6 characters), you will be given a suggestion.

Example: If you input "ShivaniMudigaPolice", the system will suggest "ShivaniMudiga" after removing the disallowed word "Police".

Step 4: Title Generation

When generating a title, the system will take your input prompt and create a title that meets the verification rules.

Example: If you input "I want a title for my Expression Tracker project", the system will suggest "Project Expression Tracker".

Step 5: Probability Score

After verification, the system will provide a probability score indicating how likely the title is to be verified based on similarity checks. If the score is too low, modify your title and try again.
